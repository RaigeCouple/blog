A Pup & The Beach

June 4, 2023

We packed up early and made our way to Longbeach with coffee in hand and our pup excitedly riding shotgun. The beach welcomed us with a gentle breeze and open sky. We spent the day playing fetch, wandering barefoot through the sand, and just soaking up each other. Nothing beats that ocean air and the happiness of a sandy-snouted dog.
